Not Just Code.
Real Infrastructure.

Software camps are everywhere. But understanding the metal—the servers, the networks, and the physical deployment of AI—is reserved for elite institutions.

We change that. By giving students access to our Garage Data Center (GDC), we demystify the cloud and teach true systems engineering.

Our Programs

Applied learning experiences bridging the gap between theory and reality.

AI Infrastructure

Direct access to GPU clusters. Students learn to rack servers, configure networks, and deploy models on bare metal.

Robotics & Vision

Building autonomous systems. From soldering circuits to programming computer vision for real-time navigation.

Systems Engineering

Linux administration, containerization (Docker/K8s), and security. Preparing the workforce for the data center boom.
